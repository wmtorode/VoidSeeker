# Privacy Policy for VoidSeeker

**Effective date:** June 11, 2026

VoidSeeker is a Discord bot that provides server moderation, anti-spam, OCR-assisted review, administrative tools, and related server-management features. This Privacy Policy explains what information the bot may process, how that information is used, and how you can request access to or deletion of data.

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the bot operator on Discord: **jamie_wolf**.

## Information the Bot May Process

VoidSeeker may process information available through Discord and information provided through bot commands or server configuration. Depending on how a Discord server configures and uses the bot, this may include:

- Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and message IDs.
- Discord usernames, display names, mentions, and membership events needed for moderation or access-control features.
- Message content when needed to detect spam, respond to commands, process bot mentions, apply moderation rules, or perform OCR-related checks.
- Server configuration, including moderation settings, authorized users, immune roles, honeypot channel configuration, and banned terms.
- OCR-related request or processing data when messages or attachments are reviewed by the bot's OCR features.
- Operational logs, including bot startup events, errors, command handling, moderation events, and troubleshooting information.

## How the Bot Uses Information

VoidSeeker uses data only to provide and maintain its Discord bot features, including:

- Detecting and responding to spam or suspected bot activity.
- Enforcing moderation settings configured by server administrators.
- Managing bot commands and server-specific settings.
- Processing OCR-related moderation workflows.
- Maintaining authorized-user and role-based access controls.
- Troubleshooting errors, maintaining reliability, and protecting the bot and Discord servers from abuse.

## Data Sharing and Sale

VoidSeeker does **not** sell user data.

VoidSeeker does **not** share user data with third parties for advertising, marketing, profiling, resale, or unrelated commercial purposes.

Data may be processed through Discord because the bot operates on the Discord platform. Discord's own collection, processing, and retention of data is governed by Discord's policies and is outside the bot operator's control.

## Data Storage and Retention

VoidSeeker may store server configuration so the bot can operate correctly across restarts and continue enforcing server settings.

Operational logs may be retained for troubleshooting, abuse prevention, and security purposes. Logs are rotated to limit their size, but some historical log data may remain for a limited time.

User-specific moderation data is retained only when the bot takes a moderation or administrative action involving that user. This data is retained unless deletion is requested, and it is used for the purpose of supporting moderation, administrative review, abuse prevention, and related server-safety functions.

## Data Deletion and Access Requests

Users or server administrators may request access to, correction of, or deletion of data associated with them or their server by contacting **jamie_wolf** on Discord.

Deletion requests will be honored when reasonably possible. Some data may be retained if required by law, Discord platform requirements, or if deletion would interfere with server administrator configuration choices.

## Server Administrator Responsibilities

Server administrators who invite and configure VoidSeeker are responsible for informing their server members that the bot is present and may process messages, member information, and moderation-related data as described in this policy.

Administrators should configure the bot only in ways that are appropriate for their community and applicable rules.

## Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect bot data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

If you believe bot data has been accessed or used improperly, contact **jamie_wolf** on Discord.

## Children's Privacy

VoidSeeker is intended for use within Discord servers and is subject to Discord's terms and age requirements. The bot is not designed to knowingly collect information from children outside the normal operation of Discord server moderation features.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the bot's features, operations, or legal requirements. Updated versions should be posted with a new effective date.
